Combining high threat response experience
with data science techniques.

 

research focused

The mission of the Barger Street Group is to conduct in-depth research to provide evidence driven insights and best practices to the first responder and emergency management community.

High Threat Incidents are not a new phenomenon. By using data from past incidents we help first responders better prepare, train, and respond to these threats.

By utilizing data science techniques we cull the signal from the noise to produce evidence based best practices and insights for the front line first responders.

How do we help

Critical Incident Debriefs

We provide first responders, emergency management, and government officials with a tailored debrief of lessons learned from a critical incident to guide preparedness, training, and response.

First Responder Recovery

We are advocates and peer resources for first reponders who answer the call for high threat incidents.
